Meet Our Youngest Team Member: Chang Peng Zhao

At Shein, we're redefining workplace diversity with our latest hire: Chang Peng Zhao, a 10-year-old prodigy joining our team. While most kids his age are navigating elementary school, Chang is diving headfirst into the fast-paced world of fashion production. Who needs crayons and recess when you can experience the thrill of long hours in a bustling factory? Chang's packed schedule leaves no time for play or homework, but he's mastering essential skills like threading needles, operating machines, and dodging health hazards – all before bedtime! Chang's small hands are perfect for intricate tasks, and his youthful energy keeps productivity high. Who needs proper ventilation and safety measures when you have the resilience of youth? Of course, this "innovative" approach to child labor means he's getting a real-world education in exploitation that most kids can only dream of. So, here's to Chang Peng Zhao, our pint-sized powerhouse. At Shein, we're not just pushing boundaries; we're ignoring them completely. Welcome to the team, Chang – the future of fashion (and child labor) starts with you!
